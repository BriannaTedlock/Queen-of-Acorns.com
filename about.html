MEET THE QUEEN

Meet Bailey


Hi! My name is Bailey, creator and owner of Queen of Acorns. I started working in the hospitality industry as a waitress 12 years ago. Since then I have found myself behind the bar for the majority of my career, with a few years of restaurant management under my belt. I ultimately wanted to start my own business due to the lack of customer service and appreciation I was noticing, not only in these corporate company positions I always found myself in, but also in my own experiences outside of work.



With the values I uphold in my life - integrity, equality, responsibility, gratitude, happiness, resourcefulness, determination - I knew I could bring the best services in my field at the best prices on the market. As our mission statement reiterates, the environment that is created surrounding Queen of Acorns is of the utmost importance to myself and my team as well. We take great pride and responsibility in promoting a safe, secure, comfortable, and entertaining space that is sure to be felt by all in the vicinity.



Our Mission

Queen of Acorns strives to provide excellent and honest mobile drink and bar services at our customer’s events. Our customer care is unmatched and guarantee you will recieve nothing less than superb service. Our team promotes responsible drinking and aims to create a unique enviornment that is safe, comfortable, and fun for all.

Specialty cocktails are crafted from locally sourced, organic ingredients and freshly pressed juices. Beer and wine are taste-tested and hand selected by our team to provide our customers with the best products on the market.
